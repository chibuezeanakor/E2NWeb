Volunteer Manager

 Connecting chairtable organizations to donors, volunteers, and recipients through our app.

Automate Coordinating Your Volunteers

The volunteer manager is the interface targeted at existing charitable organizations.  The mobile app is designed to bring together donors, drivers, and recipients; the manager is designed to allow organizations to oversee that process via a web-based application.

We plan to have the manager available at the same time our app launches later this year.

Features

  • Automate existing processes for donation drop off/pickup, and other charity events. Tracks donors, volunteers, and receipients, seeing who can bring specific goods and who needs specific goods in an integrated interface.
  • Set up traditional charity events like food drives. While we are primarily focused on on-demand donations, we don't want to eliminate proven processes either, just make them easier for everyone.
  • Match volunteers for events based on their established preferences, using smart logic. Also this same logic can be used when someone needs to cancel. This saves the organizer from manual coordination of back and forth emails, phone calls, etc. to find volunteers.
  • Track individual donations for compliance purposes (to receive county/state/federal funding, etc.).
  • Track the affiliation and vetting status of donors, volunteers, and recipients (active/inactive/approved/refused).

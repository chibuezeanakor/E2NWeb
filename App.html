Traditional Donations

If you have ever been on the giving or receiving end of charitable donations, it probably went something like this:

  • A donor visits a charity drive and drops off the goods they are donating. 
  • A recipient comes to the drive, spells out what they need, and the organization volunteers sort through what they have and give what they can to the recipient.

It's a proven process, but it still has problems:

  • The two-step process does not work well for perishable items that require immediate delivery.
  • The organizations need personnel, resources, and budgets dedicated just to running that process.
  • They require volunteers to be physically present at charity events, which takes sizeable chunks of time away from their personal lives. The commitment required for the success of these events often discourage participation.
  • It usually requires donors and recipients to physically come to the drive to give or receive the donation. While it may work in most cases, they still have to adjust their schedules, which may not always be an option.

How We're Different

Our program addresses these challenges with a technology-based platform. Using a mobile app backed by cloud services, the system brings together the donors, the recipients, and the volunteers in a single interface. This makes the process more convenient and timely for everyone involved.

Some of the particular benefits include:

  • Immediate delivery of perishable items as well as other goods – “ordering” excess food or toys becomes like having a pizza delivered.
  • Enables existing charitable organizations to automate their processes.
  • Reduces the burden on donors, volunteers, and recipients, making it possible for people to participate who wouldn’t have before.
  • Recipients get donations without being required to be physically present at charitable events.
  • Smart search technology finds the best matches of donors, volunteers, and recipients based on the donated items and locations of those involved, reducing the time and effort for everyone.
  • The app tracks donations, giving a year-end report for tax deductions.

We plan to have Android and iPhone versions of our app available for use later this year.
